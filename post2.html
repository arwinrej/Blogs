Building Better Habits, One Leaf at a Time

Big transformations are usually made from small repetitions. A ten-minute reading routine, a short daily stretch, or writing one paragraph each morning can become powerful over time.

Think of habit change like gardening: consistency matters more than intensity. You don't need to do everything today. You need to return tomorrow.

Start with one behavior so small it feels almost too easy. Protect it for two weeks. Then expand naturally.
